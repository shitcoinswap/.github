# Welcome to Shitcoin, Inc.

Shitcoin, Inc. is the company behind Shitcoin Swap, and in-development open source brokerage platform, capable of natively interacting with blockchain networks and smart contracts, as well as plugging into traditional finance APIs.

It's very early in development, but feel free to check out a demo at [shitcoinswap.com](https://www.shitcoinswap.com), or contribute to the project over at [shitcoinswap/platform](https://github.com/shitcoinswap/platform).
